The Cable Clash

Thread live wire across the arena floor and outfox the network bruisers before they trash the main event.

Arena Briefing

Your champion holds the cobalt corner while the broadcast hub flickers red across the ring. Route conductive cable segments to complete the circuit without letting the roaming rivals smash unsecured lines. Keep an eye on their telegraphed moves—anticipation is half the fight.

  • Circuit goal: Extend the cable from the cobalt corner to the broadcast hub without breaking the line.
  • Rival tells: The Diagonal Dropper slides two diagonals each turn; the Rope Rush streaks straight.
  • AC tiles: Alternating current plates demand switching between straight and corner segments.
  • Main-event slam: Closing the loop stuns adjacent rivals for one turn and locks in your wiring.

Controls

Place segment
Select a piece, then click an empty tile adjacent to your live line.
Rotate
Press R or use the rotate button to spin the selected segment.
Advance turn
Press Enter or hit the Resolve Turn button to let rivals move.

Rival Movements

  • Diagonal Dropper: Slides two diagonal steps toward the hub each turn.
  • Rope Rush: Charges two straight tiles toward the champion corner.
  • Stunned rivals skip their next move and shed the stun at the following resolve.
