Graveyard Dispatch

The path to the ancient burial ground winds through toppled headstones, rotted timber, and freshly stirred graves. The air is thick with fog and whispers. Every step upward invites the restless dead to lunge from the dark.

  • Auto-scroll ascent: The ridge never stops climbing. If the fog overtakes you, your nerve crumbles.
  • Crumbling footing: Rotten boards snap after a moment of weight. Keep moving or fall with them.
  • Reanimated threats: Carrion beasts telegraph a hiss before charging. Slip past or take the hit.
  • Effigy shortcuts: Narrow, haunted ladders guard glowing effigies. Grab one to double score gains for a brief window.
