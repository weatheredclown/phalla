Beauty Shop Briefing

The Saturday rush never lets up. Clients settle into the dryers with a hand-written Style Ticket that lists every move in order. You have seconds to grab the right tool, finish the phase, and wave them out glowing before the next cousin slides into the chair.

  • Setup ➜ Action ➜ Wrap: Seat a client, study their ticket, then work the tray one step at a time. A new face hops in the queue the moment a chair opens.
  • Queue pressure: Early shifts feature a single, gentle trim. Within minutes, three chairs will buzz at once with four-step demands and patience meters falling like hot rollers.
  • Risky gossip: A juicy update freezes every timer for five whole seconds—but it might leave you so distracted that the next tool you grab misfires automatically.
  • Feedback & flair: Scissors crack, powder pops, hairspray hisses, and happy clients leave the shop in a pastel sparkle shower.
