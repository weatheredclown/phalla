Second Star Flight (Re-issue)

Harvest Lost Shadows with a focused lantern beam, divert the Pixie Dust torrent, and fuel the Flight Meter before the darkness swallows Neverland whole.

Stage Notes

The board is cloaked in twilight. Shadows slip between alleyways while the Pixie Dust conduits lie dormant. Sweep the lantern to reveal each Lost Shadow, lock it down before it dissolves, and siphon the released dust toward the Flight Meter to clear the unreachable sky tiles. Fail to keep the dust flowing and the night clamps back down.

  • Lantern sweeps: Ignite the light to reveal tiles for a short burst. Only illuminated cells can snag the elusive shadows.
  • Shadow cadence: Pieces drift every few beats and lose cohesion over time—if they dissolve, you lose the dust surge until they reform.
  • Pixie Dust flow: Captured shadows vent dust into the reservoir. Spend it segment by segment to draw a glowing trail from the Pixie Well to the Flight Gate.
  • Think Happy Thoughts: Freeze the board for five seconds to line up a perfect sweep. Use it when the grid is most crowded.

Flight Intel

Each captured shadow adds Pixie Dust to the reservoir. Spending dust to extend the flow trail boosts the Flight Meter; close the loop at the Flight Gate to trigger a Soaring Burst that supercharges the meter and clears the board for the next wave.
