Sink the prototype research sub through a collapsing trench system, balancing oxygen, hull integrity, and dwindling fuel while alien bioluminescence flickers beyond the viewport.

Bathypelagic Briefing

Deepcore has been lowered into the Cayman Abyssal fold, tether snapped and guidance offline. The only way out is through the trench, past crushing pressure ridges, thermal vents, and living currents that shove back at every nudge of your thrusters.

  • Track the descent: Depth is the score. The farther you ride the trench, the brighter the arcade banner sings.
  • Three fragile meters: Hull fractures from every scrape, oxygen bleeds as time stretches, and fuel is the lifeline for precise adjustments.
  • Power Burst dilemma: Override the thruster governor to slam free of a rip current, but brace for wild drift, massive fuel loss, and stressed plating.

Ease into the first kilometers, map the narrow passes, and log critical moments in the mission console. When the run ends, the recovery crew needs the depth report and every decision that saved or doomed the sub.
