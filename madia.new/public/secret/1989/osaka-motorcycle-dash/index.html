Osaka Motorcycle Dash

Thread Osaka's neon corridors, shield the witness, and juggle aggression against survival while rival crews paint the pavement with danger.

Chase Briefing

The witness has one shot to clear the Namba district alive. Track them through a three-stage gauntlet that starts on the spacious Mido-suji on-ramp, squeezes through the Dotonbori back alleys, and finishes with a skyway sprint across the Umeda express bridges. Traffic density, gang aggression, and environmental traps stack with each phase—your proximity discipline is the only thing holding the ride together.

  1. Setup: Wide lanes and a forgiving proximity window help you sync with the witness and bank Boost.
  2. Chase: Lantern-lit alleys tighten the route. Rival riders flank aggressively and the Loss of Contact penalty accelerates.
  3. Finale: Elevated expressways flood with convoys and construction zones. Only precise boosts and well-timed disables can carve a path.
  • Distance is the headline: Every meter the witness survives feeds the score and the arcade leaderboard. Stall out and the run stops cold.
  • Bikes don't heal: Collisions shred Bike Health. Aggressive clears earn Protector bonuses, but lingering in the pack leads to sparks and smoke.
  • Proximity economy: Riding the witness's neon wake refuels Boost and calms the HUD glow. Drift wide and the Loss of Contact penalty screams, draining health and alerting the gangs.
  • Particles & FX: Osaka's skyline blurs by with synthwave trails, screen shake, and spark bursts whenever you catch metal.

Controls

Lean / strafe
Press W, A, S, D or the arrow keys to reposition your bike.
Boost
Hold Shift or press Boost to spend fuel for extra speed while the meter is above 20%.
Non-lethal disabler
Tap Space or press Disabler Pulse to stun nearby gang riders. Works best inside the proximity halo.
Reset
Press R or choose Reset Run to stand the bikes back up.
