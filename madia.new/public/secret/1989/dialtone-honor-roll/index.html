Briefing from Rufus

The presentation is in hours, dudes. Famous guests are dropping from the time stream inside chrono capsules that keep their arrival order locked. Sketch a Phone Booth flow trail, capture them mid-fall, and stack each capsule inside the correct era wing before they drift into tomorrow.

  • Historical Echo Capsules: Each capsule is a two-person stack with a unique horizontal drift pattern. They keep their order—no rotations, no swaps.
  • Phone Booth Flow: Plot a continuous trail from the rooftop entry. When a capsule’s base touches the lit path, it warps to your trail’s finale in the same stack order.
  • Context Queue: Complete the headlining stack recipe by aligning the listed duos inside the highlighted era bay. Nail the arrangement to bank the combo.
  • Misroutes: Capsules that land outside an era wing gum up the timeline and raise the panic meter.
