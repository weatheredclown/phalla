Carry the ballad from a whispered rehearsal to a dazzling finale by nailing every soaring note.

Performance Notes

This cabinet translates the power ballad into a single-lane rhythm showcase. Notes descend toward the spotlight—tap the beat, hold the sustained phrases, and ride the emotional swells to keep the crowd on its feet.

  • Phased staging: Verse warm-ups ease you in, the first chorus unlocks held notes, the bridge hits with tight runs, and the final chorus floods the arena lights.
  • Applause meter: Build momentum with accurate hits to raise the audience response. Dropped notes dim the room and reset your combo.
  • Crescendo moments: Ace the marquee holds to trigger a spotlight surge, doubling every point you earn while it shines.
