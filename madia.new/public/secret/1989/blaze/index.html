Level 45 · 1989 Arcade

Paper Trail Blaze

Shepherd the governor's bill through the capitol maze while siphoning scandal into cold archives.

Legislative Briefing

The bill is prepped on the Governor's Desk and the signing chamber is waiting up north. Blaze Starr keeps feeding scandal into the press line—divert it into containment archives before it splashes onto the paper trail.

  • Bill objective: Build a continuous conduit from the Governor's Desk to the Signed Law dais.
  • Scandal control: Channel each Scandal Point from Blaze's parlor into an archive chute. Crossing a bill conduit corrupts the legislation.
  • Political Capital: Earn 3 Capital whenever a bill is signed. Spending 1 Capital contains each Scandal Point that reaches an archive.
  • Victory: Deliver two clean bills before Capital dries up.

Controls

Place conduit
Select a conduit type, choose Bill or Scandal mode, then click a tile.
Rotate piece
Press R or the rotate button to cycle orientations.
Advance flow
Press Enter or the Advance Flow button to resolve a turn.
Clear tile
Use the Clear button then click a placed conduit to remove it.

Capitol Floor

Select a tile to begin the paper trail.
