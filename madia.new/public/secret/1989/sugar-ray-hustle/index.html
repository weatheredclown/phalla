Speakeasy Briefing

The Rose Room is humming, the floor packed with night owls, and the house crew is rolling gilded dice for anyone brave enough to challenge. Bankrupt the table one shark at a time by calling their moves at the exact moment the glow hits. Rely on rhythm, not reason—the dice are just stage dressing.

  • Pot Pressure: Each round starts with a fresh pot. Hover too long and the window slams shut.
  • Reaction Windows: Three concentric rings tighten around the Challenge button—Late is a push, Good pays the pot, Perfect doubles the take.
  • Swagger Meter: Run consecutive Perfects to fill your swagger. When the meter blazes, spend it to slow time for one critical round.
  • Opponents: The deeper you dance into the night, the faster the ring shrinks and the harsher the miss penalty becomes.
