Backyard Briefing

The shrink ray misfire stranded the squad half an inch tall at the edge of the lawn. Their beacon can ping the attic receiver, but only if the crew survives a dozen lawn hazards with ant ally Gauss in formation. Steward dew rations, strike when the sprinkler cycle dips, and protect the ant bond before the scorpion sweep finds them.

  • Dew supply: Hydration keeps the micro suits sealed. Sprinting too often or ignoring mist refills will crack the visors.
  • Gauss loyalty: Your ant cavalry surges the team forward but bolts if morale drops. Feed crumbs to rebuild trust before calling another ride.
  • Lawn danger: Rising sprinkler pressure, bee shadows, and roving scorpions raise the alert meter. If it maxes, the mission ends instantly.

Plot twelve decisive actions to cross the yard. Mix stealth crawls with daring ant charges, then brace for random lawn events that can help or harm. Log your best run to the arcade leaderboard once the beacon syncs with the attic.
